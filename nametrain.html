All aboard the Name Train

Add a name to the front or back of the train.
Example: Tracy Morgan Freeman
  • If the name works with other names, it's a connector.
  • If the name is at the front of the train and is no one's last name, it's the engine.
  • If the name is at the back of the train and is no one's first name, it's the caboose.
  • See how long you can make the train before you have an engine and a caboose.
